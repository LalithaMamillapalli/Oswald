LP Gas Overview

LPG is by far the most versatile, portable, clean, accessible and efficient energy source which is readily available to consumers around the world and used in thousands of applications. Some of the sectors served by LPG are as follows:
•Hospitality
•Industries
•Power generation
•Household Cooking
•Farming
•Heating
LPG is a gas easily stored in liquid form in small, medium or large containers. We at Brothers Gas provide versatile fuel backed by optimized solutions to meet the needs of our customers from last 20+ years. Enhanced LPG Solutions: From a single product LPG, Brothers Gas started diversifying in Enhanced LPG Solutions for specialized applications. Brothers Gas has invested in state of art Aerosol Plant to produce Odorless Gas and caters to various industries like Perfume, Cosmetic, etc. In 2010 Brothers Gas started its 1st LPG additive filling facility to provide alternative and enhanced products for applications like Metal Cutting, Brazing, Heating and Auto LPG solutions like Forklift Gas.

INDUSTRIAL Gas Overview

The air we breathe is a mixture of various gases, each with its own unique properties that are invaluable for industrial process and medical usages. The principal gases are nitrogen, oxygen, carbon dioxide, argon, hydrogen, helium and acetylene; although a huge variety of gases and mixtures are available. Some of the sectors served by Industrial Gases are as follows: Chemicals Glass Metal Food & Beverages Ship Repair & Maintenance Electronics Hospitality Paper Construction & Infrastructure Medical Laborites Oil & Gas Welding & Metal Fabrication A wide range of gases can be transported to users in a number of different ways, for example in cylinders under very high pressure or in special vessels designed to hold cryogenically liquefied gases at extremely low temperatures. The volume of gases purity levels and delivery intervals will vary depending on the utilization. We at Brothers Gas have been providing Industrial Gas solutions from 2010 which started with only distribution of cylinders. With continuous growth at the end of 2014 a state of the art Industrial Gas Plant equipped to provide Industrial, Medical, Specialty, Calibration and Ultra High Purity Gases was added to the portfolio of Brothers Gas. Furthering diversification led to the opening of one of the only few Ammonia Filling Plants in the region by the end of 2015
